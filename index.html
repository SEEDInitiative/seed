About SEED

Built by students who were once figuring it out too

SEED — Sprouting Educational Empowerment & Development — started as a simple idea: the hardest part of applying to university or chasing an opportunity abroad usually isn't talent. It's not knowing where to begin, not having someone to check an essay, or not having anyone who's translated the process before.

We're a new initiative, and we're upfront about that. What we lack in years, we make up for in how recently we lived through the exact deadlines, forms, and second-guessing our students are facing now.

Our founding team has worked on essay mentorship, volunteer coordination, and Arabic–English translation for students navigating gap years, applications, and scholarships — and SEED is where we're bringing that experience together in one place.

We're not trying to be a big institution. We're trying to be the person who answers your message and actually helps. SEED Initiative, Founding Team

SEED Roots

Ongoing

Our flagship 1:1 mentorship program, built exclusively for Syrian students — the one SEED first launched with. Pairs students with dedicated mentors for intensive application strategy.

  • Bridges local potential with global opportunity
  • Where SEED's founding impact began

SEED Scholar

Ongoing

An academic incubator for students who want to go deep — literature reviews, research methodology, and publication-grade papers, guided by expert mentors.

  • No lab required, just rigorous inquiry
  • Builds real research and writing skills
